Home

Glaucoma, a group of eye diseases, silently damages the optic nerve, often due to pressure buildup within the eye. This pressure imbalance can lead to progressive vision loss and eventual blindness if left untreated. Early detection is crucial as glaucoma, particularly the most common form, develops slowly with minimal initial symptoms. Regular eye exams are key to catching vision loss before it becomes significant. While there's no cure, early diagnosis and treatment can help prevent further damage and protect your sight.

Vision

Our project tackles glaucoma, a silent thief of sight, by leveraging the power of artificial intelligence (AI) to analyze retinal images. This approach goes beyond traditional methods, enabling us to objectively identify glaucoma risk factors within the intricate details of the retina. By predicting the possibility of glaucoma earlier, we empower healthcare professionals to intervene sooner, potentially preventing vision loss altogether. This translates to improved patient outcomes, as early detection allows doctors to tailor preventative measures and treatment plans for at-risk individuals.

Project Aim

Our project aims to predict glaucoma risk using cutting-edge deep learning algorithms applied to retinal images. This non-invasive approach offers the potential for earlier and more accurate detection compared to traditional methods. By identifying individuals at risk, we empower both patients and healthcare professionals to take proactive steps towards preserving vision.

•Predict glaucoma risk using advanced technology
•Empower patients and healthcare professionals with early detection.

A Word of Caution: Self-Diagnosis is Not Recommended

While this project aims to raise awareness, it's important to remember that self-diagnosis is not recommended. If you experience any vision changes or suspect you might be at risk, schedule a comprehensive eye exam with a qualified ophthalmologist.
